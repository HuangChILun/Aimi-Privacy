Welcome to Aimi. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our service to you:

1.1 Account Information

  • Google Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture from your Google account
  • Guest Identifiers: If you use the app without signing in, we generate a unique guest ID to track your usage and provide services
  • Device Information: Platform type (iOS/Android), app version, and device identifiers

1.2 User Profile & Preferences

  • Language preference (English, Chinese, Japanese)
  • Preferred communication tone and style
  • Favorite topics and conversation taboos
  • Addressing rules and interaction preferences

1.3 Chat & Conversation Data

  • Messages: All messages you send to AI characters and their responses
  • Conversation History: Complete chat logs with timestamps
  • Conversation Summaries: AI-generated summaries of your conversations for context continuity
  • Character Profiles: Custom characters you create, including names, personalities, backstories, and visual preferences
  • Story Progress: Your progress in interactive visual novel stories, including choices made, flags unlocked, and save game data

1.4 Voice & Audio Data

  • Voice Input (Local Processing Only): When you use voice input, your voice is processed locally on your device using the built-in system speech recognition (iOS/Android native). The audio recording is converted to text on your device and is NOT uploaded to our servers. Only the resulting text message is sent to our backend for AI processing.
  • Character Voice Audio (Generated): Character voice responses are generated using ElevenLabs voice synthesis service (via our backend). Only the text content of character responses is sent to ElevenLabs; your personal voice data is never shared.
  • Audio Cache: Generated character voice audio files are cached locally on your device for up to 7 days for performance optimization
üîí Privacy Notice: Your voice recordings are NEVER uploaded to our servers or any third party. Voice input is processed entirely on your device using system-native speech recognition. Only the text output is transmitted.

1.5 Usage & Analytics Data

  • Number of chat messages sent (for quota enforcement)
  • Number of voice synthesis requests
  • Number of character portrait generation requests
  • Feature usage patterns and interaction timestamps
  • Session duration and frequency
  • App performance metrics and error logs

1.6 Subscription & Payment Data

  • Subscription Status: Your current subscription plan (Free, Premium, etc.)
  • Purchase History: Records of subscription purchases and upgrades (processed by Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat)
  • Entitlements: Features and quotas available based on your subscription
  • Note: We do not store credit card or payment information. All payment processing is handled by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat

2.4 Communication

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage (On Your Device)

The following data is stored locally on your device in the app's secure sandbox:

  • AsyncStorage: Character profiles, conversation history, user preferences, story progress, settings
  • File System Cache: Generated voice audio files (automatically deleted after 7 days)
  • Authentication Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens for session management

4.2 Cloud Storage (Backend Servers)

The following data is stored on our backend servers located in the United States:

  • Account information and authentication credentials
  • Conversation history and summaries
  • Usage statistics and quota tracking
  • Subscription status and entitlements

4.3 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication: Secure token-based authentication with automatic expiration and refresh
  • Access Control: User data is isolated and accessible only to authorized services
  • Code Obfuscation: Android builds use ProGuard for code protection
  • Regular Updates: We maintain security patches and dependency updates
‚ö†Ô∏è Security Notice: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

4.4 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Audio Cache: Locally cached voice files are automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Backups: Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes

5. Device Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions:

5.1 iOS Permissions

  • Microphone Access (NSMicrophoneUsageDescription): "Aimi needs access to your microphone for voice input."
    • Purpose: Enable voice-to-text input feature for conversing with characters
  • Speech Recognition (NSSpeechRecognitionUsageDescription): "Aimi needs access to speech recognition to convert your voice to text."
    • Purpose: Process voice input and convert to text messages

5.2 Android Permissions

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Record audio from microphone for voice input
  • INTERNET: Connect to backend services for AI processing and voice generation
  • com.android.vending.BILLING: Process in-app purchases and subscriptions through Google Play
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Import/export character and conversation data
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS: Adjust audio playback for character voices
  • VIBRATE: Provide haptic feedback for interactions
Note: You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, but this may limit app functionality.
