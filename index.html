1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our service to you:

1.1 Account Information

  • Google Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we collect:
    • Email address
    • Google User ID (unique identifier, also known as the "sub" claim in the ID token)
  • Apple Account Data: When you sign in with Apple, we collect:
    • Email address (which may be Apple's private relay email if you choose "Hide My Email")
    • Apple User ID (unique identifier, also known as the "sub" claim in the ID token)
  • Device Information: Platform type (iOS/Android), app version, and device identifiers
  • Push Notification Token (Optional): If you enable push notifications, we may collect your Expo Push Token to send you notifications about new messages, story updates, or important service announcements. This token is stored only if you grant notification permissions and can be removed by disabling notifications in your device settings.

1.2 User Profile & Preferences

  • Language preference (English, Chinese, Japanese)
  • Preferred communication tone and style
  • Favorite topics and conversation taboos
  • Addressing rules and interaction preferences

1.3 Chat & Conversation Data

  • Messages: All messages you send to AI characters and their responses
  • Conversation History: Complete chat logs with timestamps
  • Conversation Summaries: AI-generated summaries of your conversations for context continuity
  • Character Profiles: Custom characters you create, including names, personalities, backstories, and visual preferences
  • Story Progress: Your progress in interactive visual novel stories, including choices made, flags unlocked, and save game data

1.4 Voice & Audio Data

  • Voice Input (Processed by Platform Provider): When you use voice input, we use the expo-speech-recognition library which relies on native platform speech recognition APIs:
    • iOS: Your voice audio is processed by Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer service, which may transmit audio to Apple's servers for speech-to-text conversion
    • Android: Your voice audio is processed by Google's Speech Recognition service, which may transmit audio to Google's servers for speech-to-text conversion
    • On-Device Option: Both platforms support on-device recognition (if language packs are downloaded), which processes audio locally without server transmission
    • Aimi Backend: Your voice audio is NEVER sent to Aimi's backend servers. Only the resulting text message (after conversion by Apple/Google) is transmitted to our backend for AI processing
  • Character Voice Audio (Generated): Character voice responses are generated using ElevenLabs voice synthesis service (via our backend). Only the text content of character responses is sent to ElevenLabs; your personal voice data is never shared with ElevenLabs
  • Audio Cache: Generated character voice audio files are cached locally on your device for up to 7 days for performance optimization
üîí Privacy Notice: Your voice audio is processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android) speech recognition services, not by Aimi. Your voice recordings are NEVER uploaded to Aimi's servers. Only the text output from Apple/Google's speech recognition is transmitted to our backend for AI conversation processing.

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage (On Your Device)

The following data is stored locally on your device in the app's secure sandbox:

  • AsyncStorage: Character profiles, conversation history, user preferences, story progress, settings
  • File System Cache: Generated voice audio files (automatically deleted after 7 days)
  • Authentication Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens for session management

4.2 Cloud Storage (Backend Servers)

The following data is stored on our backend servers located in the United States:

  • Account information and authentication credentials
  • Conversation history and summaries
  • Usage statistics and quota tracking
  • Subscription status and entitlements

4.3 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication: Secure token-based authentication with automatic expiration and refresh
  • Access Control: User data is isolated and accessible only to authorized services
  • Code Obfuscation: Android builds use ProGuard for code protection
  • Regular Updates: We maintain security patches and dependency updates
‚ö†Ô∏è Security Notice: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

4.4 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active so we can provide the service to you
  • Account Deletion: You can request account deletion through the in-app feature or by contacting us. After we verify your deletion request, we will delete your personal account data (including email, user profile, conversation history linked to that account, and subscription status) within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain records
  • Audio Cache: Locally cached voice files are automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Backups: System backups may continue to store copies of deleted data for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes, after which those copies are automatically removed
  • Abuse & Safety Records: We may retain certain de-identified records (such as fraud detection logs, abuse reports, or safety-related data) that are no longer linked to your identity. These records cannot be used to personally identify you and are maintained solely for platform safety and legal compliance

5. Device Permissions

  • Microphone Access (NSMicrophoneUsageDescription): "Aimi needs access to your microphone for voice input."
