1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
================================================================================

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our service:

1.1 ACCOUNT INFORMATION
- Google Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we collect:
  * Email address
  * Google User ID (unique identifier, also known as the "sub" claim in the
    ID token)

- Apple Account Data: When you sign in with Apple, we collect:
  * Email address (which may be Apple's private relay email if you choose
    "Hide My Email")
  * Apple User ID (unique identifier, also known as the "sub" claim in the
    ID token)

- Device Information: Platform type (iOS/Android), app version, device
  identifiers

- Push Notification Token (Optional): If you enable push notifications, we
  may collect your Expo Push Token to send you notifications about new
  messages, story updates, or important service announcements. This token is
  stored only if you grant notification permissions and can be removed by
  disabling notifications in your device settings.

1.2 USER PROFILE & PREFERENCES
- Language preference (English, Chinese, Japanese)
- Preferred communication tone and style
- Favorite topics and conversation taboos
- Addressing rules and interaction preferences

1.3 CHAT & CONVERSATION DATA
- Messages: All messages you send to AI characters and their responses
- Conversation History: Complete chat logs with timestamps
- Conversation Summaries: AI-generated summaries for context continuity
- Character Profiles: Custom characters you create (names, personalities,
  backstories, visual preferences)
- Story Progress: Progress in interactive visual novel stories, including
  choices made and save game data

1.4 VOICE & AUDIO DATA
- Voice Input (Processed by Platform Provider): When you use voice input, we
  use the expo-speech-recognition library which relies on native platform
  speech recognition APIs:

  * iOS: Your voice audio is processed by Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer service,
    which may transmit audio to Apple's servers for speech-to-text conversion

  * Android: Your voice audio is processed by Google's Speech Recognition
    service, which may transmit audio to Google's servers for speech-to-text
    conversion

  * On-Device Option: Both platforms support on-device recognition (if
    language packs are downloaded), which processes audio locally without
    server transmission

  * Aimi Backend: Your voice audio is NEVER sent to Aimi's backend servers.
    Only the resulting text message (after conversion by Apple/Google) is
    transmitted to our backend for AI processing

- Character Voice Audio (Generated): Character voice responses are generated
  using ElevenLabs voice synthesis service (via our backend). Only the text
  content of character responses is sent to ElevenLabs; your personal voice
  data is never shared with ElevenLabs

- Audio Cache: Generated character voice audio files are cached locally on
  your device for up to 7 days for performance optimization

üîí PRIVACY NOTICE: Your voice audio is processed by Apple (iOS) or Google
(Android) speech recognition services, not by Aimi. Your voice recordings are
NEVER uploaded to Aimi's servers. Only the text output from Apple/Google's
speech recognition is transmitted to our backend for AI conversation
processing.

1.5 USAGE & ANALYTICS DATA
- Number of chat messages, voice synthesis, and portrait generation requests
- Feature usage patterns and interaction timestamps
- Session duration and frequency
- App performance metrics and error logs

1.6 SUBSCRIPTION & PAYMENT DATA
- Subscription Status: Current subscription plan (Free, Premium, etc.)
- Purchase History: Subscription purchases and upgrades (processed by Apple,
  Google, and RevenueCat)
- Entitlements: Features and quotas based on subscription
- Note: We do not store credit card or payment information

We integrate with the following third-party services. Your data may be shared

3.1 GOOGLE AUTHENTICATION
Purpose: User authentication and account creation
Data Shared: Google ID token, email address, Google User ID (sub claim)
SDK: @react-native-google-signin/google-signin
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 APPLE SIGN IN
Purpose: User authentication and account creation
Data Shared: Apple ID token, email address (or private relay email), Apple
User ID (sub claim)
Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3.3 APPLE SPEECH RECOGNITION (iOS)
Purpose: Convert user voice input to text
Data Shared: Voice audio recordings (when using cloud-based recognition)
SDK: expo-speech-recognition (uses iOS SFSpeechRecognizer API)
On-Device Option: Available if language packs are downloaded
Data Flow: Voice audio goes directly from your device to Apple's servers (not
through Aimi's backend)
Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3.4 GOOGLE SPEECH RECOGNITION (Android)
Purpose: Convert user voice input to text
Data Shared: Voice audio recordings (when using cloud-based recognition)
SDK: expo-speech-recognition (uses Android SpeechRecognizer API)
On-Device Option: Available via Android System Intelligence if language packs
are downloaded
Data Flow: Voice audio goes directly from your device to Google's servers (not
through Aimi's backend)
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.5 REVENUECAT (Subscription Management)
Purpose: Manage in-app purchases and subscriptions
Data Shared: User ID, subscription status, purchase events, platform
information
SDK: react-native-purchases (v9.5.4)
Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3.6 AI LANGUAGE MODEL PROVIDER (OpenAI)
Purpose: Generate AI character responses to your messages
Provider: OpenAI API (accessed via our backend server)

Data Shared with OpenAI:
- Your chat messages (text only, converted from voice by Apple/Google if you
  use voice input)
- Character profiles and settings you've created
- Conversation context and history for continuity
- User preferences (language, tone, communication style)

Important Notes:
- Your voice audio is NOT shared: If you use voice input, your audio is
  processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android) and converted to text before
  reaching our servers. Only the text is sent to OpenAI

- Data retention by OpenAI: OpenAI may temporarily retain API request data
  (including your messages and conversation context) for up to 30 days for
  abuse detection and security purposes. Under certain legal requirements
  (such as court orders), this data may be retained for longer periods to
  comply with legal obligations

- Not used for AI training: According to OpenAI's API terms, data sent via
  API is not used to train their models unless you explicitly opt-in

Processing: All requests are proxied through our backend server; your personal
API credentials are never exposed
Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy/

3.7 ELEVENLABS (Character Voice Synthesis)
Purpose: Convert AI-generated character response text into realistic voice
audio

Data Shared with ElevenLabs: AI-generated character response text (created by
the LLM) and voice ID selection for the character

Important Clarifications:
- NOT for user voice: ElevenLabs is used to create the character's voice, not
  to process your voice input

- Your voice is never sent to ElevenLabs: When you use voice input, it's
  processed by Apple/Google and converted to text. ElevenLabs only receives
  the AI's text response to synthesize the character's voice reply

- Text-to-Speech only: The flow is: AI generates text response ‚Üí ElevenLabs
  converts that text to character voice audio ‚Üí audio plays to you

Processing: All requests are proxied through our backend server
Privacy Policy: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy

3.8 BACKEND API SERVER
Location: United States
Purpose: Service coordination, authentication, usage tracking, AI processing
Data Stored: Account info, conversation history, usage statistics,
subscription status
Security: HTTPS/TLS encryption, bearer token authentication

4. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY
================================================================================

4.1 LOCAL STORAGE (On Your Device)
- AsyncStorage: Character profiles, conversations, preferences, story progress
- File System Cache: Voice audio files (auto-deleted after 7 days)
- Authentication Tokens: Access and refresh tokens

4.2 CLOUD STORAGE (Backend Servers in US)
- Account information and authentication credentials
- Conversation history and summaries
- Usage statistics and quota tracking
- Subscription status and entitlements

4.3 SECURITY MEASURES
- Encryption in Transit: HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
- Authentication: Secure token-based auth with auto-expiration
- Access Control: User data isolated to authorized services
- Code Obfuscation: Android ProGuard protection
- Regular Updates: Security patches and dependency updates

‚ö†Ô∏è SECURITY NOTICE: While we use industry-standard security measures, no
electronic storage or transmission method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee
absolute security of your information.

4.4 DATA RETENTION
- Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active so we can
  provide the service to you

- Account Deletion: You can request account deletion through the in-app
  feature or by contacting us. After we verify your deletion request, we will
  delete your personal account data (including email, user profile,
  conversation history linked to that account, and subscription status) within
  30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain records

- Audio Cache: Locally cached voice files are automatically deleted after 7
  days

- Backups: System backups may continue to store copies of deleted data for up
  to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes, after which those copies are
  automatically removed

- Abuse & Safety Records: We may retain certain de-identified records (such as
  fraud detection logs, abuse reports, or safety-related data) that are no
  longer linked to your identity. These records cannot be used to personally
  identify you and are maintained solely for platform safety and legal
  compliance
